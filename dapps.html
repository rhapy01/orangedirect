OrangeSwap DEX

OrangeSwap is a decentralized exchange and automated market maker (AMM) that allows users to swap tokens, provide liquidity, and earn yields through farming and staking. Built with a focus on low fees, fast transactions, and a user-friendly interface.

Security Features

  • Audited by Certik and PeckShield
  • Time-locked contracts for critical functions
  • Anti-MEV protection mechanisms
  • Emergency withdrawal functions

Key Benefits

  • Low trading fees (0.25%)
  • Cross-chain compatibility
  • Optimized gas efficiency
  • Governance token with staking rewards
