Welcome to the Orange Directory - where Sign's community shines!

"The Orange Directory is a place to discover projects built by people striving to be the best version of themselves through building platforms and making genuine connections." - @sign on Twitter

The Orange Dynasty is the Sign Community - ecosystem supporters and community members, currently about 800,000 strong and growing daily, who have been developing amazing projects that deserve to be showcased.

Being the best version of yourself means beating odds daily, setting goals, reaching them, and achieving status. These developers and creators aren't from yesterday. They do what they know best - sincerely and innovatively. Community members around the world have been developing various tools, games, and dapps to showcase how they are beating all odds to become the best version of themselves.

This Orange Directory exists to bring together all those amazing and beautiful initiatives so everyone can access and use any of these developments, having a place where users can find genuine links and information about each project being carried out.

This world is unfathomable, and the testimony of how these community members became the best version of themselves is worthy of emulation. We encourage all users to engage and follow these developers, mindset growers, product designers, game developers, and more on social platforms to support them.
